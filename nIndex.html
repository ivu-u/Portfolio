My Vision

Hello! I'm Jasmine, a Computational Media Major who loves basically everything about games. My goal is to create games that convey a specific experience (capturing a moment in time)... there are so many interesting things in this world! Even something as simple as the way traffic lights are optimized is something worth learning about to me.

Bonbon

My Roles: Programming Co-lead, Gameplay Programmer

Bonbon is a 3D story-driven RPG about a boy who wants to follow his dreams, and his older sister who just wants to keep him safe. The main gameplay loop consists of turn-based combat interspersed with cute dialogue scenes.

Bonbon is the project I’m currently working on through the Georgia Tech Game Development Club (VGDev)!
